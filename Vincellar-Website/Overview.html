VinCellar Overview

Here you can find out more about all the exciting features that VinCellar offers.

Overview

The most complete, and completely free, cellar management tool

No other online cellar management software offers:

Comprehensive cellar management tools & analysis, Professional scores, drink dates & reviews, Community tasting notes, and much more!

Helping make your life easier

All new purchases from Vinfolio are automatically barcoded and entered into VinCellar for free. All of your data is secure and instantly available online.

Sell Wine

VinCellar will help you make the most of your wine

Everything you'd expect from great cellar management software...and then some

Wine and vintage Label images, quantity and bottle size, physical location details, cost and purchase source to name a few

Vinfolio offers a simple, transparent and profitable way to sell your wine

Submit a list of wines to be sold and VinCellar provides an itemized price quote on eligible wines, and we'll help you with the rest

    Valuation and Analysis

    Only VinCellar provides both auction and retail price data to accurately value and monitor the wine in your collection

    Value your cellar accurately... and automatically

    Choose to value your wine cellar based on auction pricing or retail pricing, VinCellar uses our proprietary algorithm that uses both current market auction results or retail pricing data

    Analyze your cellar with robust tools and reports

    VinCellar includes charts and graphs to graphically display your entire collection by Variety, Readiness to drink, Valuation, Cost data, Gain/loss, and more
